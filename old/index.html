History

Frioland Realty and Development Corporation

Frioland Realty and Development Corporation is a company engaged in the business of providing a wide range of real estate services. The company specializes in consolidating properties and providing raw land for clients who intend to develop them into different real estate projects, ranging from residential and commercial areas to industrial zones and other similar ventures.

Together with an extensive network of PRC-Licensed Real Estate Brokers, PRC-Accredited Salespersons, Land Scouts, Specialists (Geodetic Engineers, Civil Engineers, Land Surveyors), Consultants (Land Conversion and Reclassification Experts), and professional business partners, Frioland Corp. undertakes its strategic business operations from its headquarters in Laguna (Southern Luzon) and Alabang (Metro Manila), with satellite offices and networks in Northern and Central Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao, and extends its reach to any point in the Philippines.

Formerly established as First Ten Realty in 2012 under the general real estate brokerage category, Frioland Corp. expanded its operations to a wider array of services ranging from property acquisition/disposition to raw land consolidation, different land studies, land conversion & reclassification, property due diligence & documentation, and property consultation in 2015.

Since then, the company has been working with multiple clients involved in, among others, the development of residential subdivisions, commercial properties, utility plants, renewable energy farms, and other similar ventures.
